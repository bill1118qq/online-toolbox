Discount Calculator

Quickly calculate discount prices, savings amounts, and compare deals. Supports multi-item discounts, reverse calculation, and multi-product comparison.

Discount Formulas

Sale Price = Original Price x (Discount / 10)

Savings = Original Price - Sale Price

Discount Rate = (Savings / Original Price) x 100%

Example: Original 200, 20% off -> Sale Price = 200 x 0.8 = 160, Savings = 40
